# How to Change Your Profile Picture

Your profile picture appears on your account page and may be visible to other people in your organization. You can upload a new photo or remove your current one at any time.

## Upload a New Photo

1. On your Account Centre page, find the **Edit Profile** section.
2. Click **Choose File** and select an image from your computer.
3. Click **Update Image**.

Your photo is automatically cropped to a square — the center of the image becomes the profile picture.

## Remove Your Profile Picture

If you want to remove your photo and revert to the default:

1. In the **Profile Image** section, click the **x** button on your current photo.
2. Your picture reverts to the default.

## Requirements

- **Accepted formats**: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF
- **Maximum file size**: Shown below the Choose File button (defaults to 1 MB)
- Photos are cropped to a square — content near the edges may be cut off

## Tips

- Use a square or near-square image for the best result
- Avoid very large files — smaller images upload faster
- If your upload fails, check that your file meets the size and format requirements above
